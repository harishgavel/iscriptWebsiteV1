About ISCRIPT

ISCRIPT is the short form of SCRIPT — Society for Computational Research, Innovation & Patent Technology. We exist to build a trusted ecosystem where research becomes innovation, innovation becomes IP, and IP becomes real-world solutions.

  • Join ISCRIPT
  • Collaborate With Us

Vision

To establish a strong and credible society that accelerates high-quality computational research and innovation, enables protection of intellectual property, and supports technology transfer for societal and economic impact.

Mission

  • Promote advanced R&D in Computational Science, AI, Cybersecurity, IoT, and allied technologies.
  • Provide structured support for patents, copyrights (software code), and industrial designs.
  • Build IP-driven solution portfolios and enable licensing / technology transfer.
  • Strengthen skill development through workshops, seminars, and training programs.
  • Offer advisory and consultancy to institutions, government bodies, and private organizations.

Aims & Objectives

1) Promotion of Research & Innovation

  • Promote advanced research in Computational Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, IoT, and allied technologies.
  • Create a collaborative platform for researchers, academicians, and industry experts to identify and solve critical technological challenges relevant to India and the globe.

2) Intellectual Property (IP) Facilitation

  • Facilitate protection of intellectual property generated by members, including drafting, filing, and prosecution of patents, copyrights (software code), and industrial designs.
  • Provide technical and legal guidance regarding patentability assessment, prior art search, and compliance with Section 3(k) for computer-related inventions.

3) Technology Transfer & Solutions Development

  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry by curating IP-driven solutions into market-ready portfolios.
  • Facilitate Technology Transfer (ToT) and licensing of patented technologies to industries, startups, and MSMEs.
  • Act as a nodal agency for incubating innovative ideas and transforming research prototypes into viable products.

4) Skill Development & Dissemination

  • Organize workshops, seminars, and training programs on patent drafting, IP valuation, and computational research methodologies.
  • Publish technical journals, newsletters, and white papers showcasing member innovations and solutions.

5) Advisory & Consultancy

  • Offer consultancy services to educational institutions, government bodies, and private entities on IP strategy, technology road-mapping, and R&D audits.
