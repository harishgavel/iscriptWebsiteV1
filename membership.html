Membership

Become a member of ISCRIPT (SCRIPT — Society for Computational Research, Innovation & Patent Technology) and join a growing network focused on research excellence, innovation, IP facilitation, and technology transfer.

  • Apply / Enquire
  • View Programs

Why join ISCRIPT?

  • Research collaboration: Connect with researchers, academicians, and industry professionals.
  • IP support: Guidance for patentability, prior art search, drafting, and Section 3(k) compliance.
  • Technology transfer focus: Convert prototypes and patents into deployable solutions and portfolios.
  • Skill development: Workshops, training programs, and community learning.
  • Visibility: Opportunities to feature work in newsletters, white papers, and society publications.

Membership categories (v1)

You can customize these categories later based on your Society rules. For now, this structure works well for launch.

Student Member

  • For UG/PG/PhD students
  • Access to workshops & research groups
  • Mentoring guidance for IP readiness

Academic / Research Member

  • For faculty, researchers, scholars
  • Collaborative project leadership
  • Support in publications & patent structuring

Industry / Professional Member

  • For working professionals, engineers, consultants
  • Industry problem statements & mentorship
  • ToT and licensing collaboration support

Institution / Startup Partner

  • For institutions, labs, startups, MSMEs
  • Joint research, incubation and ToT support
  • Access to IP-driven solution portfolios

Note: Membership fees, voting rights, tenure, and governance details can be added after finalizing the Society Rules & Regulations.

How to apply

  1. Send your details to iscript.society@gmail.com or use the contact form.
  2. Include your profile (student/faculty/professional), domain interests, and how you’d like to contribute.
  3. ISCRIPT will respond with the next steps for onboarding and participation.

Recommended details to share

  • Full name + affiliation
  • Membership category
  • Research/industry domain
  • Portfolio links (Google Scholar / GitHub / LinkedIn)
  • Any idea you want to explore for IP support

Priority support areas

  • Patent drafting readiness
  • Prior art search structuring
  • Section 3(k) framing and technical effect
