Aims & Objectives

ISCRIPT is established with the following aims and objectives:

  1. Promotion of Research & Innovation: Promote advanced research in Computational Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Internet of Things (IoT), and allied technologies. Create a collaborative platform to identify and solve critical technological challenges relevant to India and the globe.
  2. Intellectual Property (IP) Facilitation: Facilitate protection of intellectual property including patents, copyrights (software code), and industrial designs. Provide technical and legal guidance on patentability, prior art searches, and Section 3(k) compliance for computer-related inventions.
  3. Technology Transfer & Solutions Development: Bridge academia and industry by curating IP-driven solutions into market-ready portfolios. Enable Technology Transfer (ToT) and licensing to industries, startups, and MSMEs; incubate ideas and translate prototypes into viable products.
  4. Skill Development & Dissemination: Organize workshops, seminars, and training on patent drafting, IP valuation, and research methods. Publish journals, newsletters, and white papers showcasing member innovations.
  5. Advisory & Consultancy: Offer consultancy to educational institutions, government bodies, and private entities on IP strategy, technology road-mapping, and R&D audits.
