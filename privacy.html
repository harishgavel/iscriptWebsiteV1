Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ISCRIPT (SCRIPT — Society for Computational Research, Innovation & Patent Technology) collects, uses, and protects information shared through this website.

1. Information we may collect

  • Contact details: Name, email address, and any details you provide through forms or email.
  • Enquiry content: Messages, collaboration proposals, membership details, and other information you submit.
  • Basic technical data: Browser/device information and approximate usage metrics (if enabled by your hosting provider).

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested support (membership, IP guidance, programs, collaborations).
  • To improve our website content and communication.
  • To maintain internal records required for society operations.

3. Confidentiality and IP-sensitive submissions

Please avoid sharing highly confidential invention details via public web forms. For IP-sensitive communication, send a non-confidential summary first to iscript.society@gmail.com. If needed, further sharing can happen through appropriate confidentiality arrangements.

4. Sharing of information

ISCRIPT does not sell or rent personal data. We may share information only when:

  • It is necessary to support your request (e.g., connecting you with relevant ISCRIPT members/experts), and only with reasonable care.
  • Required by law or valid legal process.

5. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected (or as required for society operations). You may request deletion of your contact records by emailing us.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure. Please share information responsibly.
