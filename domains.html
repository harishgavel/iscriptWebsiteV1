Research & Innovations

ISCRIPT (SCRIPT — Society for Computational Research, Innovation & Patent Technology) promotes advanced research and innovation in Computational Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Internet of Things (IoT), and allied technologies. Our goal is to convert research into impactful, IP-backed solutions.

  • Join the Research Network
  • Propose a Research Problem

What we aim to do

We create a collaborative platform where researchers, academicians, and industry experts can:

  • Identify critical real-world challenges relevant to India and the global ecosystem.
  • Form multidisciplinary teams to develop solutions and prototypes.
  • Strengthen outcomes through publications, patents, and technology transfer pathways.
  • Encourage reproducible research and responsible innovation.

Focus Areas

Some key research domains supported by ISCRIPT include (and are not limited to):

Artificial Intelligence & ML

  • Explainable AI (XAI) and trustworthy AI
  • AI for security, healthcare, and governance
  • Edge AI and low-resource deployment
  • Computer vision, NLP, and multimodal systems

Cybersecurity

  • Threat detection, SOC/SIEM, security analytics
  • Vulnerability analysis and security assurance
  • Compliance automation and audit readiness
  • Privacy-preserving and secure systems design

IoT & Embedded Systems

  • IoT security and device trust frameworks
  • Smart sensing, monitoring, and automation
  • Industry 4.0 and CPS (Cyber-Physical Systems)
  • Edge-fog-cloud integration for IoT workloads

Computational Science & Allied Tech

  • Optimization, metaheuristics, and scheduling
  • High-performance computing and simulation
  • Data analytics pipelines and real-time systems
  • Applied cryptography and post-quantum readiness

How we work

ISCRIPT encourages an outcome-driven pathway—so that research does not stop at a paper or prototype.

Stage What happens Output
1. Problem Identification Collect industry/academic problems and define scope, constraints, and success metrics. Problem statement + plan
2. Research & Prototyping Design approach, build prototype, validate with datasets, benchmarks, or field trials. Prototype + evaluation
3. IP Readiness Prior art search, novelty assessment, Section 3(k) screening (if applicable), drafting strategy. Draft patent / IP plan
4. Publication & Portfolio Publish and/or bundle IP into a solution portfolio aligned to market needs. Paper / portfolio
5. Tech Transfer Enable licensing, partnerships, ToT, and incubation for real-world deployment. Industry adoption
